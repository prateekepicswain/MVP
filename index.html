Make Ventures Princeton

We enable Princeton's leading creatives, leaders, and builders to pursue entrepeneurial ventures.

About Us

MVP - Make Ventures, Princeton - is Princeton’s premier startup accelerator. We support 3 founding teams of 4-5 people as they develop their product or idea into a business over the course of twelve weeks. By the end of the program. our companies will be positioned for investments and hyper-growth. Our program features:

  • Weekly dinners with founders and thought leaders
  • Weekly design, engineering, and product office hours
  • A Demo Day to venture capitalist at the end of 12 weeks
  • Fundraising support to fuel your growth
  • Intro and connections to the Princeton Alumni Network and beyond
  • Instant access to Princeton’s entrepreneurial resources: TigerLaunch, HackPrinceton PSICOMP
